## Reporting a Vulnerability
- Email: security@example.com
- Subject line: "MLP Vulnerability Report"
- Include steps to reproduce, affected components, logs, and any mitigation ideas.
- Expect an acknowledgment within 3 business days and a status update every 7 days until resolution.
